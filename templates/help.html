Getting Started

If you're new to our application, here are some steps to get you started:

  1. Visit the home page to explore the main features.
  2. Use the 'Get Statistics' to retrieve channel statistics by entering a channel name.
  3. Go to the 'Get Video Detail' to download video details by specifying a channel name.
  4. Visit the 'Get Prediction' to make predictions based on views and comments.
