About Us

Welcome to our application! We're excited to share some information about our project and its purpose.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide users with valuable insights and data related to YouTube channels and videos. We aim to make it easier for users to access channel statistics, video details, and even make predictions based on that data.

Features

Our application offers the following key features:

  • Channel Statistics: Retrieve channel statistics by entering the channel name.
  • Video Details: Download video details by specifying a channel name.
  • Predictions: Make predictions based on views and comments using machine learning models.
